# Sentry for ODC

Sentry for OutSystems Developer Cloud (ODC) is a security and compliance add-on that extends [ODC's built-in platform security](security.md) with advanced protection, compliance certifications, and advanced self-service security configuration. With Sentry for ODC, you get elevated security capabilities for building and operating mission-critical applications.

## Capabilities

Sentry for ODC includes the following capabilities:

* **Compliance certifications**: Attestation and documentation for regulatory standards including HIPAA and PCI DSS. For more information, refer to [HIPAA compliance with ODC](hipaa.md) and [PCI compliance with ODC](pci.md). For a current list of all certifications and attestations, refer to [OutSystems ODC with Sentry security](https://security.outsystems.com/?product=odcwithsentry).

* **IP filtering**: Restrict access to your apps based on IP addresses. Create rules at the app level, within app groups, or by stage to control which traffic reaches your deployed assets. For more information, refer to [Manage IP filters](../manage-platform-app-lifecycle/filter-ip.md).

* **Audit trail streaming**: Stream platform audit logs to your preferred security information and event management (SIEM) tool for real-time threat detection, centralized security operations, and extended log retention. For more information, refer to [Stream audit trail logs](../monitor-and-troubleshoot/audit-trail/audit-trail-streaming.md).

* **Intrusion detection**: Advanced detection safeguards your apps and data against known, unknown, and undisclosed threats by monitoring for anomalous patterns and activities. This applies to shared platform infrastructure. Application runtime isn't covered.

* **Malware scanning**: Continuous scanning detects unexpected, unauthorized, or malicious activity across the platform infrastructure. Application runtime isn't covered.

## Check your subscription

To confirm that Sentry for ODC is active for your organization, check the [Subscription console](../manage-platform-app-lifecycle/subscription-console.md). If Sentry for ODC isn't listed, contact your OutSystems account manager.
