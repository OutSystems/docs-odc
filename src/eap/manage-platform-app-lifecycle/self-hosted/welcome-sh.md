# Self hosted ODC EAP

Welcome to the Self-hosted Early Access Program (EAP). This space is dedicated exclusively to participating customers and provides the information you need to explore, install, and configure your Self-hosted ODC.

Because this is an early access initiative, the documentation reflects work in progress. It will evolve as the product matures and as we incorporate your feedback throughout the program. During the early access period, documentation is available only in English. Please do not share or distribute this material outside your organization. Thank you for your cooperation.

Only EAP participants have access to the Self-hosted EAP documentation. If you're in the EAP and one of your members doesn't have access to the documentation, please ensure they're added to your [OutSystems Customer Portal](https://www.outsystems.com/csportal/Home).

## Program goals and scope

The main objective of this program is to validate the readiness of Self-hosted for general availability (GA). To achieve that, we’ll work closely with participants to:

* Test real-world use cases to confirm product-market fit and value.  
* Gather feedback to refine features and improve the overall user experience.  
* Identify and address friction points in product workflows.  
* Establish baseline adoption and engagement metrics to guide future scalability.  
* Assess technical stability and readiness.

<div class="info" markdown="1">

At this stage, the EAP focuses on validating the installation process and deployment of apps to self-hosted stages. Data fabric, AI models, and Agent Workbench aren't available yet.

</div>

## Benefits for participants

As an EAP participant, you’ll get:

* Early access to the Self-hosted ODC.  
* Access to documentation and implementation guidance to support your setup.  
* A direct feedback channel with the product team, giving you an opportunity to help shape the roadmap.

Use the navigation menu to explore architecture details, installation instructions, and configuration requirements.
