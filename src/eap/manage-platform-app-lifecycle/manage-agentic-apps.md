# Manage agentic apps from the ODC Portal

The ODC Portal's **Agents** page lists every agentic app in your tenant. Use it to open an existing agentic app and review the agents inside, or to create a new agentic app. To open the page, sign in to the ODC Portal and select **Agents** from the **Create** group.

## What you can do from this page

The **Agents** page supports two primary tasks:

* **Open an existing agentic app.** Select a card on the grid to open the agentic app and review the agents it contains.
* **Create a new agentic app.** Select **Create agentic app** to start the creation flow. The flow continues in ODC Studio, so you need ODC Studio installed to finish. For step-by-step guidance, refer to [Creating an agent in ODC Studio](../building-apps/build-ai-powered-apps/create-agent.md).

For background on agentic apps and the AI agents they contain, refer to [Agentic apps in ODC](../building-apps/build-ai-powered-apps/agentic-apps.md).
